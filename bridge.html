We (Alice, Ben, Carla and Denis), have to cross the dark bridge. This is not possible without a lantern. We only have one lantern, but the light is so weak that only two people can cross the bridge safely at the same time. The candle in the lantern has almost burned out. Therefore we have to cross the bridge as quickly as possible. But we can't run at the same speed. Alice takes 5 minutes, Ben 10 minutes, Carla 20 minutes and Denis 25 minutes for one way. Tell us the fastest solution to get everyone across the bridge. Please!
This is a riddle from the 9th century,
see S. X. Levmores und Elizabeth Early Cooks
